About the Gathering

Where culture becomes medicine

La Cultura Cura is a culturally rooted, family-centered wellness gathering that honors ancestral healing practices while integrating preventative health education and trauma-informed principles. A bilingual event for the whole family — Body, Mind, and Spirit.

This is not a drop-in fair. It is a guided, structured community experience — woven together by workshops, ceremonial círculos, live music and movement, a shared community meal, and all-day booths and activities.

Now in its third year — built by the community, for the community. Un día dedicado a la curación, la conexión y la comunidad.
